Thanks for downloading Lightbeam!

  1. Open Lightbeam by clicking the logo in the Add-on bar.

  2. Lightbeam starts recording connections as soon as it's installed. If you see a blank screen, vist a couple of sites, then check back!

    Or you can leave Lightbeam open in the background, and watch while you browse as it gets populated with sites.

  3. Consider contributing data to Mozilla! We'll use it to study the tracking landscape and develop new privacy-protecting technologies for the web.

    You can opt in to sharing your data by flipping the "Contribute Data" toggle in the top right corner to "On".

Questions? Ideas? Found a bug?

  1. Email our mailing list: lightbeam@mozilla.org
  2. File an issue on Github
  3. Write a review on Mozilla's Addons page
